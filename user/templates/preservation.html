当书签已更新，因服务器故障无法上传时的补救措施

整个步骤大概需要3-5分钟，且只能在PC端浏览器上进行，其他平台无法补救。步骤如下：

  1. 在浏览器书签页面，同时按下Ctrl、Shift、I（大写字母i)三个键
  2. 找到" 查看器" ,将鼠标指针移动到该位置并点击，见图1
  3. 找到右侧的鼠标指针符号,将鼠标指针移动到该位置并点击，见图1
  4. 将鼠标指针移动到" 根文件夹" (根文件夹目录，你可以修改它的名字，就是最上面的文件夹名称)，点击它
  5. 在屏幕下方，对应的位置将会被高亮显示，见图2
  6. 找到上方的" < div id= " ifm " >" ，将鼠标指针移动到它上面，并点击它；然后点击鼠标右键，弹出快捷菜单，见图3
  7. 将鼠标指针移动到" 复制" 上，并单击后续的" 内部HTML" ，此时书签内容已经保存到你的剪贴板中了
  8. 打开任意文本编辑器，比如windows中的记事本，新建文本文件，将内容粘贴到文件中并保存
  9. 待后续服务器正常后，将保存的文件进行上传
  10. 若服务器持续故障，请联系网站管理员1575658778@qq.com
Remedy for upload failure

The entire step takes about 3-5 minutes and can only be performed on a PC-side browser, other platforms not available. Here are the steps:

  1. On the browser bookmark page, press Ctrl, Shift, I (capital letter i) at the same time
  2. Find the "Inspector", move the mouse pointer to that location and click, as shown in Figure 1
  3. Find mouse pointer symbol, move the mouse pointer to that position and click, as shown in Figure 1
  4. Move the mouse pointer to the "root folder" (this is any root folder directory that you rename, which is the top folder name), click on it
  5. At the bottom of the screen, the corresponding position will be highlighted, as shown in Figure 2
  6. Find the above" <div id="ifm">" move the mouse pointer over it and click on it, and right-click to pop up the shortcut menu, see Figure 3
  7. Move the mouse pointer over "copy" and click the subsequent "Inner HTML". Now,the bookmark content is saved to your clipboard
  8. Open any text editor, such as Notepad in Windows, create a new text file, paste content to it and save
  9. When the server is available, upload the saved file
  10. If the server continues to fail, contact the webmaster at 1575658778@qq.com
