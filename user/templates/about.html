本网站用于网络链接的收集与分享，可以通过手机浏览器在线操作，从而实现跨平台（可以在PC端、手机端、平板电脑等任何包含web浏览器的工具）的管理，且不必依赖浏览器。同时也可以方便的将自己的书签分享给他人。

优势
  • 跨平台、跨浏览器，不对任何厂商的工具产生依赖，实现真正的自主
  • 可分享，或者说对所有人可见
  • 免费
  • 操作简单、支持上传已有的（即从浏览器导出的html格式的）书签
劣势
  • 书签的所有内容都是公开的，可以被其他人看见
  • 因为是通过网络存储的，稳定性没有浏览器自带的书签好，甚至可能因为服务器或网络问题导致更新的内容保存失败，补救措施
  • 需要登录，有一定的时间消耗；如果仅浏览书签，可以直接访问链接而无需登录
  • 对于非书签的所有者，有广告

This website is used for the collection and sharing of web links and can be operated online through any web browser thus, cross-platform and independent of specific browser. It is also convenient to share your bookmarks with others.

Advantage
  • Can be shared, or visible to everyone
  • Free
  • Simple, support uploading existing (i.e. html format exported from browser) bookmarks
Disadvantage
  • Everything in a bookmark is public and can be seen by others.
  • Because it is stored over the network, the stability is not as good as the browser's own bookmarks, and server or network problems may cause the content to fail to save. remedy
  • Need to log in, certain amount of time to consume; if just visit bookmarks without change, you can visit the shared address without signing in
  • For bookmark visitors( not the owner), there are ads
